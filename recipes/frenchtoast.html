French Toast

Description:

A simple and easy french toast recipe for your delicious breakfast to energize your day.

Estimated time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Brown Sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Salted butter
  • Eggs
  • Maple Syrup

Steps:

  1. Whisk the eggs together.
  2. Whisk the sugar, vanilla and cinnamon into the eggs.
  3. Pour the ingredient into the whole milk and whisk it together.
  4. Pour it into a shallow baking baking dish.
  5. Soak the bread on each side for 10-15 seconds.
  6. Melt 1/2 tablespoon of salted butter on the griddle or pan to cook the soaked bread. Cook until it turn golden-brown.
  7. Serve it with maple syrup, whipped cream, fruits and cinnamon honey-butter.
