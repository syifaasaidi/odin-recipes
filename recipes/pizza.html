Margherita Pizza

Description:

A simple and easy homemade pizza recipe for your day.

Estimated time: 1 hour 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • White whole wheat flour and all-purpose flour
  • Maple syrup
  • Warm water
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Mozarella
  • Basil leaves
  • Pizza sauce
  • Garlic
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Dried Oregano

Steps:

  1. Mix white whole wheat flour with yeast, a little maple syrup, warm water, salt and olive oil.
  2. As your homemade pizza dough rises, make sure to keep it covered.
  3. Pulse your tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, red pper flakes and a pinch of dried oregano in the food processor to make sauces.
  4. Stretch and spread sauces on your dough while preheat the oven 500'F.
  5. Top with the mozarella and tomatoes.
  6. Bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  7. Remove from oven and top with basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes.
