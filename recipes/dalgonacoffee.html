Dalgona Coffee

Description:

A simple and yummmy drinks.

Estimated time: 5 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Granulated sugar
  • Instant coffee
  • Boiling water
  • Hand Mixer

Steps:

  1. Add two tablespoons of sugar, instant coffee and 2 tablespoon of boiling water in a bowl.
  2. Whip it using handmixer around 2 to 5 minutes..
  3. Pour some of milk in your favorite mug.
  4. Scoop the whipped coffee and place it on top of your milk.
